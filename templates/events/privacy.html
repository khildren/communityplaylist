We collect only what's needed to run the site: account info you provide (username, email), event submissions, and standard server logs (IP, browser, page visited). We do not sell your data.

We use essential cookies only — session authentication and CSRF protection. No third-party ad tracking cookies are set. You can clear cookies at any time in your browser settings.
