Features

Everything built into Community Playlist — submitted by the community, for the community.

v0.4 — Portland, OR
Video Channel v0.4 New

Portland artists, venues, and crews now have a live video channel — PDXTV — local-only. Past sets, VODs, and live streams all in one place.

📺

PDXTV Archive Channel New

Tap 📺 VIDEO in the bottom player bar to open PDXTV. YouTube videos from connected artist, venue, and promoter channels shuffle into the queue — weighted toward artists with upcoming shows.

🔴

Live Twitch Streams New

Artists, venues, and promoters can connect a Twitch channel. When they go live, their stream surfaces at the top of the video queue automatically — live badge, viewer count included.

🎬

Twitch VODs

Past Twitch broadcasts (VODs) join the archive channel alongside YouTube content. Everything stays local — only connected PDX artists, venues, and crews.

⚡

Auto-advance & shuffle

The channel plays continuously — YouTube auto-advances on track end, live streams surface first, and the queue is weighted toward artists playing shows soon. Channel-change TV static effect included.

Profiles & follows v0.3

Community Playlist now has accounts, profiles, and a follow system — all free, no tracking, no ads.

👤 Your profile LIVE @handle, pronouns, bio, links. Public profile at /u/@handle/. Email-verified accounts. 🏛 Venue LIVE Verify your space, manage your calendar feed, and surface every event you host — automatically. 🎤 Artist LIVE Claim your events, list your genres, link your socials. Share tracks via Google Drive. Connect YouTube or Twitch for the video channel. 📣 Promoter / Crew LIVE Collectives, sound systems, labels — showcase your events and connect YouTube or Twitch for the live video channel.
⭐

Follow artists, venues & neighborhoods New

Follow anything on the site with one click. Your dashboard shows everything you're following. A personal RSS feed at /u/@handle/feed/ streams upcoming events from your follows — works in any feed reader.

Discover events
≡

List, Map & Hybrid views

Browse upcoming events as a calm scrollable list, explore by map for discovery, or use hybrid mode for side-by-side research. View preference is saved between sessions.

🔍

Search & smart filters Updated

Full-text search across title, description, and location. Filter by genre, category, neighborhood, date range, free-only, and now — distance (within 15 / 30 / 60 miles of Portland). Fully mobile-friendly with a collapsible filter panel.

📍

Neighborhood pages New

Every major Portland neighborhood has its own page at /neighborhoods/ — a wiki history blurb, upcoming events, and a community board scoped to that area. Post potholes, neighborhood watch notes, mutual aid, or just say hi. No account needed to post.

🗺

Interactive map Updated

Hover a list card to highlight its marker. Click a marker to scroll to that event. Category-colored dots — music purple, bike green, food gold, and more. Flyer thumbnails in popups. All powered by OpenStreetMap — no Google, no tracking.

🚗

Idle map autopilot New

Leave the page idle and the map comes alive — it cycles through events one by one, animating a real driving route between each stop along Portland streets, zooming into each venue, opening an info card, and updating the weather to that event's forecast. Touch anything to take back control; autopilot resumes after 2 minutes.

⚡

Happening Now New

Events currently in progress are pinned in a collapsible strip at the top of the list. They disappear automatically when they end — no refresh needed.

🌤

Live weather New

Current Portland weather is shown in the header — temperature, condition, wind speed, and feels-like. During autopilot, the pill updates to show the forecast for each event's location and time.

📅

Calendar subscribe

Subscribe to a live iCal feed in Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or any calendar app. Build a custom filtered feed →

Submit & manage
✦

Submit an event

Address auto-geocodes with a live map preview. Neighborhood is auto-detected. Genre tags, artist tags (auto-detected from the event title), multiple links, flyer photo — all supported.

🎤

Artist tagging New

Tag performing artists on any event. Type a name and it searches our database, then MusicBrainz if not found. Artists are auto-detected from event titles (e.g. "ft.", "w/", "presents").

🔁

Recurring events New

Mark an event as recurring — weekly, monthly, every 3rd Thursday, annually, and more. Future instances generate automatically. Resident artists are tagged on every instance.

🔑

Claim your event

Register with the email you used to submit and your events are automatically claimed. Edit details and track approval from your dashboard.

Event pages
🔁

Recurring series browser New

Every event page shows all dates in its series — past and upcoming — so you can jump to any instance at a glance. Works for both submitted recurring events and auto-imported iCal series.

🌹

The Flyer

The first photo uploaded is featured as the flyer — full-width, orange-bordered. Additional photos form a gallery.

📍

Smart location links

Addresses link to OpenStreetMap directions. Calendar icon adds the event to Google Calendar with one tap.

📸

Community photo uploads

Anyone can add recap photos after an event happens. Captions and submitter credit supported.

👁

View counts New

Event and venue pages quietly track how many times they've been viewed — one count per session, no cookies, no tracking. Helps surface what's resonating.

The PDX event net

Community Playlist automatically pulls events from across Portland so nothing slips through the cracks. All imports are geocoded, categorized, and genre-tagged automatically.

📡

Venue iCal feeds

Dozens of PDX venues, collectives, and promoters publish iCal feeds. We import them twice a week and auto-detect new ones monthly.

🌿

Food & mutual aid events New

Free breakfasts, community fridges, farmers markets, and food banks are now in the feed — Blanchet House, Rosehip Medic Collective, PSU Farmers Market, and more. Filter by the Food category to find them.

🎛

19hz.info — PNW electronic music New

The OG Pacific Northwest electronic music calendar, run by the community since 1997. We pull Oregon shows automatically — raves, warehouse parties, club nights, and ambient sets that never make it to Ticketmaster. 19hz.info →

🎸

MusicBrainz

The open, community-maintained music encyclopedia. We pull Portland concert data and use it for artist search and tagging. Free, nonprofit. Donate to MetaBrainz →

📅

Calagator

Portland's own open-source community calendar, covering tech meetups, civic events, and workshops since 2008. We import their events automatically. calagator.org →

🗺

OpenStreetMap & Nominatim

All maps and geocoding run on OpenStreetMap — community-built, freely editable, no tracking. About OSM →

Community
📋

Community Board New

A lightweight message board at /board/ — no account needed, just a name. Post topics, reply to threads, and find Aid & Mutual Aid resources. Recurring events get their own auto-created thread. Pinned topics stay at the top.

🌿

Aid & Mutual Aid ticker New

A scrolling banner at the top of the page surfaces active mutual aid messages — free breakfasts, community fridges, resource links. Text is managed in the admin and links to the Aid board.

💬

Discord community

Event chatter and community hangout in #website-communications. New events ping the server for real-time visibility.

🐛

Bug reports & feature requests

Found something broken? Have an idea? Use the 🐛 and 💡 buttons in the nav bar — they go straight to GitHub Issues. Public, tracked, and versioned. Community Playlist is open source.

🌹

Visitor counter

A quiet count of unique visitors — one per session, no tracking, no ads, no data sold.
