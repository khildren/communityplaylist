Then paste the URL from the share dialog above (the regular spreadsheet URL is fine — we convert it).

Column headers (order flexible, case-insensitive):
Artist | Title | Label | Year | Format | Condition | Price SOL | Notes | YouTube
YouTube column is optional — paste a YouTube URL to pin a specific video (overrides Discogs auto-lookup).
