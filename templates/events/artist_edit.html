🎼 MusicBrainz

MusicBrainz is the open encyclopedia that Spotify, Apple Music, Last.fm, and ASCAP all pull from. Linking your profile here does a few things for you:

  • Your name shows up accurately in genre catalogues and streaming metadata
  • ASCAP, BMI, and SOCAN use it to track live performances — helps if you're registered
  • Releases, aliases, and collaborations all get indexed under one canonical ID
  • A verified badge appears on your Community Playlist profile
