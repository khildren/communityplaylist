What we are

Community Playlist is a free, ad-free event calendar and artist directory for Portland, OR — built to keep local culture visible without paywalls, algorithms, or corporate gatekeeping.

We list music events, track local artists and crews, map venues and community spaces, and surface the underground scene that makes PDX worth living in.

No venture capital. No ads. No user data sold. Kept alive by community support and people who believe local culture deserves better than Facebook Events.
